About ant

A URI front door over the tamnd site tools.

ant gives every record across many sites one address: a resource URI like x://user/nasa or wikipedia://page/Go. The console dereferences a URI to its record, lists a resource's members, walks the link graph between records, and searches the domains that support it.

Records are cached under the data tree as JSON, JSONL and Markdown, so a second view is offline. A live fetch happens only on a cache miss or an explicit refresh.
